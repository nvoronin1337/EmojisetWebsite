Epidemiology Research


Branch of medical science that deals with investigating the factors that causes diseases/disorders to be present/absent in a society Epidemiology is the method used to find the cause of health outcomes and diseases in populations by viewing the patients collectively It is used by researchers, medical practitioners, the government and sometimes even the general population since different behaviours within society can affect the spread of certain viruses, as we have seen with COVID-19.
